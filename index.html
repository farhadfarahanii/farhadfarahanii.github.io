EquiJob: Using AI to Balance Bias in Job Descriptions

Despite strides in diversity, global employment discrimination persists. Unconscious biases infiltrate job descriptions; words like ‘dominant’ deter women, while ‘collaborative’ dissuades men. In this project with Omdena Toronto, Canada Chapter, Our goal is to employ NLP for creating an AI tool to counter bias in job descriptions.
